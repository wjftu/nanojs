# Sic Bo (With Betting)

Play Sic Bo with real betting - manage your balance and view history

### How to Play

1. **Set Initial Balance**: Click ⚙️ Settings to set your starting balance
2. **Select a chip**: Choose $10, $50, $100, or $500
3. **Place bets**: Click on betting options to add chips
4. **Click Roll Dice**: Roll all three dice
5. **Collect winnings**: Winning bets highlight in green
6. **View History**: All rolls are recorded in history

### Features

- **Custom Balance**: Set your own starting balance in Settings
- **Manual Clear**: Clear bets to return to balance (before rolling)
- **Full History**: Every roll is recorded with dice, sum, bet, payout, and profit
- **Clear History**: Manually clear all history when desired

### Payouts (Atlantic City Standard)

| Bet Type | Payout |
|----------|--------|
| Small / Big | 1:1 |
| Any Triple | 30:1 |
| Specific Triple | 180:1 |
| Specific Double | 10:1 |
| Two Dice Combo | 5:1 |
| Single (1 die) | 1:1 |
| Single (2 dice) | 2:1 |
| Single (3 dice) | 3:1 |
| Sum 4 or 17 | 60:1 |
| Sum 5 or 16 | 30:1 |
| Sum 6 or 15 | 17:1 |
| Sum 7 or 14 | 12:1 |
| Sum 8 or 13 | 8:1 |
| Sum 9-12 | 6:1 |

### Tips

- Start with Small/Big bets for ~50% win rate
- Use Settings ⚙️ to reset balance anytime
- History shows all your gameplay results
- "Clear Bets" returns unspent balance (before rolling)
